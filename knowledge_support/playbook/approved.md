# Hammer Support Playbook

Approved support answers promoted from live sessions or admin edits.

### Password reset not received

Ask the customer to check spam and confirm the email on file matches their login. If still blocked, escalate to support@hammertime.com with dealership name and contact email.





### working hours

To update your business hours or daily closures, please follow these steps:

1. Log in to Hammer at **hammertime.com**.
2. Select **Working Hours** on the left.
3. To edit **regular business hours**, make changes directly on this page.
4. To update **holiday hours**, click **Add Exception**, enter the hours, and select **Save**.

Let me know if you need further assistance!

### remove a user from my account

To remove a team member or sales rep from your Hammer account, please follow these steps:

1. Log in to Hammer at **hammertime.com**.
2. Go to **Account** in the top right corner.
3. Click on **Team**.
4. Remove the user that you no longer want to have access to your account.

If you need further assistance, please let me know! Could you also provide your dealership name and your name for our records?

### change the name of my ai

To change the name of your AI, please follow these steps:

1. Log into your Hammer account.
2. Click on **Agent Settings**.
3. Enter the new name you want in the **"Name"** field.

Once you've made the change, it should update accordingly. Let me know if you need further assistance!

### how do i change the number the ai provides to my customers

To change the number the AI provides to your customers, please follow these steps:

1. Log into your Hammer account.
2. Click on **Business Details**.
3. Enter the new number you want in "Assistance Phone Number."
4. Press the blue save button in the top right corner.

If you need further assistance, feel free to ask!
