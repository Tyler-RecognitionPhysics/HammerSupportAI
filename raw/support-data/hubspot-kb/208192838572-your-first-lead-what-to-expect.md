Your First Lead: What to Expect

 Your first HammerAI conversation may look slightly different from traditional CRM leads.
 Hammer prioritizes speed of response , which means:

 Customers may receive a message immediately

 Conversations may already be active when viewed

 Additional customer information may appear later

 Hammer begins engagement using the information available at the moment the customer reaches out and gathers more details naturally during conversation.
 This helps prevent missed opportunities caused by delayed responses.
 Seeing multiple updates on the same lead is normal and does not typically indicate duplicate outreach.
 You May See Required Actions Appear
 After Hammer begins messaging, a Required Action may appear in your dashboard/CRM/Email indicating where your team can continue the conversation.
 This helps your staff quickly identify which customers are ready for human follow-up.
