Welcome to HammerAI: How it Works

 HammerAI helps your dealership respond instantly to customer inquiries so no opportunity is missed.
 Hammer connects to your existing lead sources and begins conversations automatically using SMS and supported chat channels.
 Depending on your setup, Hammer can respond to leads and messages received from:

 Connected lead providers (CarGurus, Carfax, TrueCar, AutoTrader, etc.)

 Facebook Business Page Messenger and Lead Forms

 Supported CRM lead-forwarding integrations

 Hammer Website Chat

 OfferUp messaging

 Hammer tracking phone numbers used in your marketing

 When a customer reaches out, Hammer:

 Receives the inquiry

 Starts an immediate conversation

 Continues follow-up via text message

 Notifies your dealership team

 Sends conversation updates back into your CRM

 Hammer works alongside your CRM — it does not replace it .
 Dealerships with a connected CRM can continue working leads directly inside their CRM if preferred. Hammer conversation updates are designed to merge into existing customer records so teams do not need to manage multiple systems.
 As Hammer works conversations, it assigns Required Actions inside your dashboard.
 Required Actions help your team quickly identify when human follow-up is needed, such as:

 Scheduling an appointment

 Calling a customer

 Confirming availability

 Continuing a conversation

 Your team can join or take over conversations at any time to respond from the same number.

 Important to Know
 HammerAI is primarily a lead conversion tool , meaning it works and updates leads after they are created rather than generating new leads itself (except for Website Chat and Hammer Ads products).
 If you'd like to verify everything is working, see Testing Your Setup .
