Why Do I See Multiple Updates for the Same Customer?

 Short Answer
 Seeing multiple updates for the same customer is expected behavior in HammerAI.
 Hammer works customer conversations continuously and sends updates as conversations progress so your team always knows when follow-up is needed.
 These are conversation updates , not new leads.

 Why This Happens
 HammerAI is a lead conversion tool , meaning it continues working a customer after the original lead is received.
 As customers reply or new information is gathered, Hammer may:

 Continue texting the customer

 Ask additional questions

 Schedule next steps

 Follow up automatically

 Each meaningful interaction may generate a new Required Action or update so your dealership knows where to step in.

 Hammer Works Conversations Separately From Your CRM
 Hammer manages conversations independently from your CRM.
 As conversations progress, Hammer sends updates back into your CRM to keep your team informed.
 Depending on your CRM’s deduplication settings, these updates may appear as:

 Updates to an existing customer record ✅

 Separate lead entries ⚠️

 Both scenarios are normal and depend on CRM configuration.

 Why Multiple Required Actions May Appear
 You may notice Required Actions updating or reappearing when:

 The customer replies again

 Hammer gathers new information

 The conversation changes direction

 Follow-up timing occurs

 This ensures your team always sees the latest recommended next step .
 It does not mean Hammer contacted the customer multiple times unnecessarily.

 Common Example
 A customer may:

 Submit a lead

 Receive an immediate response from Hammer

 Reply later with a question

 Request pricing or availability

 Each step updates the conversation and may create a new Required Action so your team knows when to engage.

 This Does NOT Mean
 ❌ Hammer created duplicate outreach ❌ The customer was contacted repeatedly ❌ Multiple leads were generated intentionally
 It means the conversation is active and progressing.

 How to Work These Updates
 When you receive a new lead update:
 ✅ Review the summary for last known update to take action.
 [If working out of CRM: Summary is in the Comments section of the merged duplicate lead]
 Optional, but recommended next steps:
 ✅ Open the Conversation Link ✅ Review the latest conversation ✅ Continue the discussion with the customer from the same number or disable/re-enable the AI & followups as needed.

 When Multiple Records Appear in Your CRM
 Some CRMs merge conversation updates automatically, while others require matching customer information before merging records.
 If records do not merge, your CRM’s deduplication sensitivity may need adjustment.
 This behavior is controlled by the CRM and not by Hammer.

 Best Practice
 Treat Required Actions as your dealership’s active follow-up queue rather than focusing on individual lead entries.
 Hammer ensures conversations stay active — Required Actions show when your team should step in.

 Need Help?
 If you believe updates are appearing unexpectedly, Support can review conversation history quickly using a customer phone number or example lead.
