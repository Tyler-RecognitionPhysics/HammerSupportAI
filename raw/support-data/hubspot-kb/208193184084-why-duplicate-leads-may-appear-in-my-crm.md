Why Duplicate Leads May Appear in My CRM

 Short Answer
 Duplicate leads appearing in your CRM are usually caused by how CRM systems merge customer information — not by HammerAI sending multiple updates.
 Hammer works conversations over time and sends conversation updates back to your CRM so your team stays informed.
 Depending on your CRM settings, these updates may appear as separate entries.

 How Hammer Sends Information to Your CRM
 HammerAI is a lead conversion tool , meaning it works customer conversations after a lead is received.
 As conversations progress, Hammer sends updates to your CRM that may include:

 New contact details

 Conversation Activity

 Follow-up progress

 These updates help ensure your dealership always has the latest customer interaction recorded.

 Why Some CRMs Show Duplicate Records
 CRMs decide whether records merge based on matching customer information such as:

 First and last name

 Phone number

 Email address

 If enough matching information is not available at the time the update is received, the CRM may temporarily create a new record instead of merging automatically.
 This behavior varies by CRM and dealership configuration.

 Common Situations Where This Can Occur
 Limited Customer Information
 Some conversations begin with only a phone number or first name (for example, Website Chat or SMS inquiries from Tracking Numbers).
 If additional details are provided later, the CRM may treat updates as separate entries before merging.

 Multiple Customer Touchpoints
 A customer may:

 Submit a website form

 Start a chat conversation

 Text a tracking number

 These interactions can arrive through different paths but still represent the same customer.

 CRM Deduplication Settings
 Each CRM controls how aggressively records merge.
 Lower merge sensitivity may result in multiple visible entries even though the conversation belongs to the same customer.

 Important to Know
 Duplicate CRM records do not mean :
 ❌ Hammer contacted the customer multiple times ❌ Multiple leads were generated intentionally ❌ Opportunities were lost
 Hammer continues working a single conversation and sending updates so your team stays informed.

 Can Duplicate Records Be Reduced?
 In many cases, yes.
 Possible improvements include:

 Adjusting CRM deduplication sensitivity

 Ensuring consistent customer contact information

 Reviewing notification and forwarding settings

 Because merge behavior is controlled by the CRM, available options vary by provider.

 Need Help?
 If you believe duplicate records are occurring unexpectedly, Hammer Support can review conversation delivery using a customer example or phone number.
