Can I Work Hammer Leads Directly in My CRM?

 Short Answer
 Yes. Dealerships can work customer conversations either inside Hammer or directly within their CRM , depending on their preferred workflow.
 Hammer is designed to support both approaches.

 How Hammer Works With Your CRM
 When a CRM is connected, HammerAI works customer conversations independently while sending conversation updates back into your CRM.
 These updates are intended to merge under the existing customer record so:

 The originally assigned salesperson remains assigned

 Leads are not re-round-robined

 Your team can continue working customers normally inside the CRM

 Two Supported Workflows
 ✅ Option 1: Work Leads Inside Your CRM
 Your team can:

 Open the customer record in the CRM

 View Hammer conversation updates

 Access the conversation summary or link to takeover or disable the AI

 Continue working the customer as usual

 Hammer acts as an engagement and follow-up layer while your CRM remains the primary workspace.

 ✅ Option 2: Work Leads Inside Hammer
 Your team can instead use Required Actions inside Hammer to:

 See customers needing attention

 Open conversations instantly

 Text customers from the same tracking number

 Disable AI when taking over

 Both workflows access the same customer conversations.

 Why Hammer Sends Updates as Duplicate Leads
 Hammer sends conversation updates designed to merge with existing CRM records rather than creating entirely new opportunities.
 This helps prevent:

 Leads being reassigned unintentionally

 Round-robin disruptions

 Sales ownership conflicts

 How these updates appear depends on your CRM’s deduplication settings.

 Which Workflow Should My Dealership Use?
 There is no required workflow.
 Many dealerships choose to:

 Managers monitor Required Actions in Hammer

 Salespeople continue working leads inside the CRM

 Others operate fully within Hammer.
 Hammer supports whichever process fits your store best. Keep in mind, Hammer does not round robin lead updates – to round-robin leads you would need to use your CRM. 

 Important to Know
 If Hammer updates are not merging as expected inside your CRM, records may appear separately. This behavior is controlled by CRM merge settings and can often be adjusted.
 Hammer Support can help review examples if needed.

 Best Practice
 Hammer manages engagement. Your CRM manages dealership operations.
 Use whichever system best supports your team’s workflow.
