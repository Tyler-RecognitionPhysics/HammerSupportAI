Contacting Hammer Support

 If you need assistance, the Hammer Support team is happy to help at hammertime.com/help
 For fastest resolution, please include:

 Dealership name

 Customer phone number or example lead

 Approximate time of issue

 Screenshots if available

 Hammer conversations and delivery events can be reviewed quickly when examples are provided.
