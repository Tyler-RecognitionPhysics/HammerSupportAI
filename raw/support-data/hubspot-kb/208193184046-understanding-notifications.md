Understanding Notifications

 Hammer uses Required Actions and Notifications to keep your dealership informed and organized.
 Required Actions appear in your dashboard and indicate when your team should review or continue a customer conversation.
 Required Actions help your dealership prioritize follow-up.
 Examples include:

 Schedule appointment

 Call prospect

 Confirm appointment

 Provide information

 Continue conversation

 These actions appear in the Prospects view inside Hammer or the comments section of your Prospect Profile within your CRM. They help guide your team toward the next step.
 Notifications alert users when activity occurs.
 Notifications may include:

 New conversations

 Phone Calls received

 Follow-up Updates

 Notifications can be delivered by:

 Email (ADF for CRMs)

 SMS (Text alerts can be toggled on/off for all new conversations started with Hammer ONLY)

 Because Hammer sends conversation updates, multiple notifications for the same customer may occur as conversations progress. These should be merged under the original customer profile within your CRM if it is set to de-duplicate your leads.
