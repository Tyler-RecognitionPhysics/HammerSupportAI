What Happens When a Lead Comes In?

 When a customer submits a lead or sends a message through a connected source, HammerAI immediately begins engagement.
 Typical flow:
 Step 1: Customer submits a lead or message Step 2: Hammer sends an instant SMS or chat response Step 3: Conversation continues automatically Step 4: Your team receives notifications Step 5: Conversation updates are sent into your CRM
 As conversations progress, Hammer creates Required Actions to guide your team on when to step in.
 You may see multiple conversation updates or Required Actions for the same customer — this is expected and ensures your team always knows the next best action.
 Most CRMs automatically merge these updates into the existing customer record depending on CRM deduplication settings.
