How HammerAI Responds to Customers

 HammerAI primarily communicates with customers through SMS text messaging , along with supported chat integrations.
 Responses are based on:

 Agent Settings

 Questions & Answers configured in your dashboard

 Customer responses during conversation

 Hammer may:

 Ask qualifying questions

 Provide vehicle information

 Encourage next steps

 Follow up automatically

 Your dealership maintains full control and may:

 Join conversations

 Disable AI responses / follow-ups

 Text customers directly from the same tracking number

 Channels Hammer Can Respond To
 ✅ SMS conversations ✅ Facebook Business Page messages ✅ Website Chat ✅ OfferUp messages

 Channels Hammer Cannot Respond To
 ❌ Personal Facebook messages ❌ Email conversations (See Switch2Text ) ❌ Leads sent in unsupported formats (such as HTML-only)
