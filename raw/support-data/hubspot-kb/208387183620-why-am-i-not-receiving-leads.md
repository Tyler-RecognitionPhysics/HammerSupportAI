Troubleshoot why new customer leads may not appear in HammerAI and how to verify successful lead delivery

Why Am I Not Receiving Leads?

 Short Answer
 If you are not seeing new leads in HammerAI, the most common cause is that a lead was never successfully delivered to Hammer.
 This usually means:
 ✅ the lead source connection needs verification ✅ routing changed inside your CRM or ad platform ✅ or testing was performed using an unsupported method
 Hammer can only respond after a lead is received in ADF/XML or through a proper chat connection.

 Why This Happens
 HammerAI does not generate leads [exception to this are the Hammer Website Chat or Hammer Ad products] . 
 Hammer responds only after customer information is submitted through a connected source such as:

 Facebook Lead Forms

 Website Chat

 Marketplace providers (CarGurus, AutoTrader, etc.)

 CRM integrations or forwarding emails

 If delivery stops upstream, Hammer will appear inactive even though the system is functioning normally.

 Step 1: Confirm a Lead Was Submitted
 First verify that a real customer inquiry occurred.
 Check:

 Facebook Lead Center

 Website chat submission

 Lead provider dashboard

 CRM inbox

 If no submission exists, Hammer will not activate.

 Step 2: Check Integration Connections
 Lead interruptions commonly occur after:

 admin permission changes

 Facebook Page disconnection

 CRM email forwarding updates

 Website updates removing chat scripts

 Even small permission changes can pause delivery.

 Step 3: Run a Valid Test Lead
 A proper Hammer test requires:
 ✅ real phone number ✅ connected lead source ✅ supported integration
 Successful tests should immediately create:

 AI response

 Conversation record

 Required Action, if applicable

 Step 4: Verify Lead Routing
 If the provider shows delivery but Hammer does not receive it, routing may have changed.
 Support can confirm delivery using:

 Customer name

 Submission time

 Lead source

 Important to Know
 Not receiving leads does not usually mean:
 ❌ Hammer stopped working ❌ Messaging failed ❌ Customers were ignored
 It almost always indicates a delivery interruption before Hammer engagement begins.

 Still Not Receiving Leads?
 Hammer Support can quickly verify connection status and restore delivery.
 Providing one recent example allows immediate investigation.
