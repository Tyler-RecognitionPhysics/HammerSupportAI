How to Test Your Setup

 You can confirm HammerAI is working by sending a test inquiry through any connected lead source.
 Recommended testing methods:

 Submit a test lead from Carfax, CarGurus, or another provider

 Send a Facebook message

 Use your Website Chat

 Text a Hammer tracking number

 Include a valid phone number when testing so Hammer can respond.
 After testing, confirm:
 ✅ Hammer responds automatically (or delayed if typing seed set) ✅ A conversation appears in your dashboard ✅ A Required Action is created (when applicable) ✅ Notifications are received to all emails ✅ CRM receives updates (if connected)
 If Hammer responds but your CRM does not update, your CRM routing or deduplication settings may need review.
