Hammer Responded but I Don’t See the Lead

 Short Answer
 If HammerAI responded to a customer but you do not see a new lead, the conversation was most likely:
 ✅ merged into an existing CRM record ✅ or your Notification settings are configured to not receive new conversations or follow-up updates

 Why This Happens
 HammerAI works customer conversations continuously rather than creating a brand-new lead every time activity occurs.
 When Hammer engages a customer, the conversation may appear in different places depending on your setup.

 Step 1: Check in Hammer
 The fastest way to locate the conversation is inside Prospects 
 Prospects show all conversations. Filters and Search are available on the right side. 
 Opening the Prospect will display the full conversation immediately.

 Step 2: Check Your CRM (If Connected)
 If your dealership uses a CRM, Hammer updates are often merged into an existing customer profile instead of creating a new lead.
 This is intentional and helps:

 Keep the originally assigned salesperson

 Prevent round-robin reassignment

 Maintain a single customer record

 Instead of a new lead, you may see a merged duplicate with:

 A note or activity update

 Conversation summary

 Hammer conversation link

 Recent customer interaction

 Your CRM may already contain the updated conversation under the original customer.

 Step 3: Check Notifications
 Hammer may successfully respond even if alerts were not received.
 Each user controls their own notification preferences, so conversations may exist without triggering a visible alert.
 Notify when a New Conversation is started : If enabled On, Hammer will send the update immediately regardless if the customer has responded [Not recommend to be enabled if your CRM does not deduplicate or if you have the Hammer webchat active]
 Notify on Follow-up Updates: If enabled On, Hammer will send a lead update when we initiate a follow-up; this does not mean the customer responded. 

 Important to Know
 Hammer responding without a new lead appearing does not mean:
 ❌ The lead was lost ❌ Hammer failed to deliver ❌ The customer was contacted incorrectly
 It usually means the conversation updated an existing opportunity.

 Hammer or CRM — Your Choice
 Dealerships can manage conversations using either:
 ✅ Required Actions inside Hammer or ✅ Existing workflows directly inside their CRM
 Both systems reflect the same customer engagement.

 Still Can’t Find the Conversation?
 Support can quickly locate activity using:

 Customer phone number

 Approximate message time

 Lead source

 Providing an example allows us to trace delivery instantly.
