*How to Activate Hammer Corp Export in DealerCenter (Updated Instructions)*
1. *Log in* to your DealerCenter account.
2. On the left-hand menu, click *Inventory*.
3. Select *Export*.
4. At the top, click the blue button *Activate New Export*.
5. A list of available export partners will appear.
 Scroll to *Hammer Corp*.
6. Click *Activate*.
7. In the pop-up window:
*Do NOT* toggle on *Call for Price*. Leave it off.
Under *Publish*, switch from *Manual* to *All Vehicles with Photos*.
Under *In*, make sure it shows *In Inventory*.
8. Click *Activate* (blue button).
9. You should now see *Hammer Corp* listed under your *Active Exports* — you’re all set!
