*Fullpath* is an automotive customer data and marketing platform that connects a dealership’s CRM, DMS, website, inventory, and ad channels into one system. It:
1. *Pulls in data* from all sources (CRM, DMS, website, ads).
2. *Identifies shoppers*, even anonymous ones, by matching online behavior to CRM records.
3. *Scores leads* based on intent and recommends follow-up actions.
4. *Automates marketing* with personalized ads, emails, texts, and retargeting.
5. *Pushes updates back* into the CRM with full reporting and attribution.
This helps dealers focus on the right leads, reduce waste, and sell more cars faster.
