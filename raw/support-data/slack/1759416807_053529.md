**U01AYS87U4R** (2025-10-02 16:16 UTC):
Since most of the accounts are brand new, TikTok begins by charging in small daily increments (e.g., $10, $10, $15). As the payments are successful, the account builds up a billing threshold. Once the threshold is established, charges will only occur when either the maximum threshold amount (e.g., $100, $150) is reached or the monthly billing date arrives. Billing dates occur once per month.

To my knowledge, there is no way to adjust the threshold or billing date, it is automatically adjusted by TikTok.
